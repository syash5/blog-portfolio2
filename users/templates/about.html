About Us

As part of its ongoing dedication and commitment to meet the growing needs of qualified engineers in the domestic and global market, Bhagwan Mahavir Education Foundation (BMEF), added newest star in its firmament in July, 2009, in the name of Bhagwan Mahaveer college of Engineering & Management (BMCEM). The institute has adequate infrastructure as per AICTE, New Delhi and Affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), New Delhi norms. The institute has well ventilated classrooms, tutorial rooms, laboratories, workshop, playground and grassy lawns. The modern laboratories are equipped with latest equipments required to provide the best hand on training to very budding engineer. The campus is surrounded by enormous greenery situated in a very peaceful and pollution free environment far away from hue and cry of city life. BMCEM is geared to nurture the intellect and personality of future generations of leaders, innovators, creators, independent thinkers and, above all, responsible global citizens. The Principal having more than 20 years experience in teaching and research leads the BMCEM as Director-Principal with a team of well qualified and dedicated staff.
